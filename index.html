GOLDEN CROSS Logistics

Medical Services

We've been servicing Nashville and surrounding countries with Non-emergency transportation since 2015. We transport members to and from doctors appointments, day centers, pharmacy stops, and Nursing Facilities. We also service transportation to Rehabilitation Centers and more. We pride ourselves with a central focus on our efficiency and courtesy to all of our members. Because we're family owned we treat everyone as if they're family but maintaining complete professionalism. We offer private pay options as well as insured services. Please contact us with any questions?

School Services

We currently partner with the local school districts and national partners to provide transportation to students in unique situations. We plan and organize transportation for students that fall under the MCkinney-Vento title, at-risk students, kids with Challenging locations, Foster kids and children in group homes. While using the most innovative technology we provide efficient and safe transportation to one of the most vulnerable populations, our children. Please contact us for more information.

Mother Services

We recently began servicing women who are both pre and post partum. Partnering with local non-profits we provide transportation to these women to and from doctors appointments. Also in partnership with the local non-profit, through this program we have unique opportunities to provide certain job opportunities to members. More details to come.
